Our Products

Select from a wide range of products for your animal needs

Doxyvicline : It is an effective formulation of broad spectrum antibiotics and vitamin C for use in poultry and livestock. The product is used in the prevention and treatment of gastrointestinal infections and chronic respiratory disease in Poultry, Calves and Pigs such as Bordetella, Campytobacter, Chlamydia, E.coli, Klebsiella, Haemophilus, Mycroplasma, Pasteuralla, Rickettsia, Salmonella, Staphylococcus and Streptococcus spp.

Tylovis : It is a combination of Tylosin Tartrate and Doxycycline Myclate. In Poultry it is used as aid in the treatment of CRD associated with Mycroplasma gallsepticum sensitive to tylosin in broilers and replacement chicken. In Turkey it is used for the treatment of infectious synovitis. In Pigs it is used for the treatment and control of swine dysentry.

Ciprogen : It is an antibiotic combo of gentamycin sulphate and ciproflaxin hydrochloride. Its unique formula makes it very effective in the treatment of a wide range of bacterial infections both in poultry and livestock.

Cocivis : It is used in the prevention and treatment of intestinal and caecal coccidiosis and bacterial diseases in poultry and other investors such as sheep, goats, pigs and rabbits.

Piperazole : It is an anti helmintic that is active against infections with roundworms (Nematodes), tape worms, lung worms in Poultry, Cattle, Horses and Pigs.

Amclo Care : It is a effective combination of antibiotics sulphonamide and vitamins used for the prevention and treatment of chronic and acute Pox-virus in poultry chickens and turkeys.

Super Viraltrin : It is a combination of an effective broad spectrum antibodies, antiviral and electrolytes formulation for use in poultry and livestock ( cattle, goat, dog etc).

V-Vitamin C : It is a combination of vitamins C & E used to boost the immunity and productivity of poultry and livestock alongside its anti-stress effects.

Tetravis : It is a broad spectrum antibiotic that controls specific diseases in Poultry, Cattle and Sheep. In Poultry it is treatment for CRD, infectious Synovitis, Airsac infection and Fowl Chlorea. It prevents chicken mortality and stress.
